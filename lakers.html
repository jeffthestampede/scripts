How much is a Lakers ticket?

When do Lakers tickets go on sale?

The availability of Lakers tickets will vary from year to year. Generally, NBA tickets become available in September preceding the season.

How much are season tickets for the Lakers?

Lakers season ticket prices will vary from year to year. They will generally become available in September.

How much were tickets for Kobe's last game?

For Kobe Bryant’s last game at the Staples Center on April 13th, 2016, get-in prices started at $717, and had a median price of $1,820. For more information, see our blog.
